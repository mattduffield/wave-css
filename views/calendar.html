wc-calendar

Binds an events array onto a date grid. Prev/Today/Next + month/week/day/agenda switching, drag to reschedule (wccalendarchange), per-event color, and "+N more" overflow. Date-only / UTC-midnight values are treated as floating dates (no timezone shift); datetimes render in the browser's local zone unless timezone is pinned.
